The Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower began being constructed on January 26th 1887, it was finished
in two years. The tower was constructed to be wind resistant so it wouldnt frail over time.
It was intended to be put together by bolts but builders noticed that rivets would expand when
they cooled down for a more secure fit. Evreyone hated the idea of the tower at first but quickley
saw how cool it was and started to like it.
